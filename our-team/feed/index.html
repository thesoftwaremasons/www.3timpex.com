DELE AYEMIBO (Ph.D)

Bamidele Ayemibo (Ph.D) is the CEO of 3T Impex Consulting Limited. He is a renowned author, international business coach, keynote speaker, International business development expert, and one of the foremost International  Trade Consultant, an International Trade Specialist (ITS), and Certified Documentary Credit Specialist (CDCS), AfCFTA Ambassador with more than a decade years of international trade experience within and outside the Banking sector.

He is the first Certified Specialist in Demand Guarantee in Nigeria and among the first ten in Africa. He holds a Masters in International Business with Law from Salford University, Manchester, United Kingdom and holds a Ph.D. In International Business & Marketing with a first-class honour at the LIGS University, Hawaii, United States of America. He conceived, designed and facilitates the training of the First Export Diploma programme and Ten other Trade Professional Development Programmes in Africa in conjunction with the American Institute of Extended Studies.

He is a member of the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) Working Group (WG) on Trade Digitalisation and Blockchain in the world, as part of the ICC Commission on Customs and Trade Facilitation. He is a facilitator at the Lagos Business School, an Export Business Mentor on the UK Aid PDF II Export Mentoring programme, and the former Chairman, of the Export Group at Lagos Chamber of Commerce & Industry.

He anchors an export radio programme on Nigerian info FM and the author of seven trade books: “Export Business Made Easy,” “Import Business Made Easy,” “Demand Guarantee Made Easy,” “Standby Letter of Credit Made Easy,” “A-Z of Export Business Financing,” “Built to Go Global,” and “Eureka! I Have Found International Buyers.”

He has trained more than 70% of the currently certified trade finance specialist in Nigeria. He set up the foremost trade consulting company in Nigeria and has a very rare combination of expertise in international trade finance, trade rules, banking background and trade execution experience. He is an avid blogger who regularly posts export-related information on www.tradeinfong.com.
